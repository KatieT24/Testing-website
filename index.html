Towns that the Tombstones appear in:

  • Darrenshome, Kentucky

The first time we came across the strange phenomenon of Three tombstones popping up in a small town of Darrenshome, Kentucky on July 24th, 2022. It was so strange that the town thought it was just a small prank set up by the local kids with nothing to do, until the Graveyard workers tried to remove the 'Fake' tombstones.

Unfortunately, the tombstones for whatever reason wouldnot be budged or moved. They had even tried to jackhammer the tombstones out of place, but have been unable to even make it move out of the way. The oldest of the Gravyeard workers in Darrenshome had mentioned that perhaps if was of supernatural origins, that the three tombstones were put there...

The same phenomenons happend in Farrows, Kansas and Hillshire, Iowa. Allthough in Hillshire there was a pattern broken. The middle gravestone as shown below, had a shallow grave with an ornate urn buried.

While these three locations show that TTT(The Three Tombstones) that keep popping up recently, there may be more than just these three locations. our team of reserchers are constantly on the lookout for this particular situation to hear from many more that recognize that these TTT's are not part of the graveyard set up, many would think that it would just be simple pranks. But what pranks would show the TTT's in the corner of the graveyards they were placed.


For those who have seen this strange happenings in your town, please visit our Contact Us page and send us your location and email adress to us so that we can send out our small resercher team to be able to see if the strange phenomenon is fake or in our worse case, real.
